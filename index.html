The Mission

The goal of this site is to promote small and upcoming businesses within the community. Businesses will be able to collaborate with each other while and introduce residents in those areas to new and rewarding services. Businesses may also be highlighted for significant contributions to the community such as being eco-friendly.

Unfortunately, many local businesses face hardships that prevent them from continuing to serve the community due to lack of support financially, socially, and directionally. Some statistics from The Zebra on the status of small businesses in America state:

  • Across the United States, over 543,000 new businesses open every month.
  • 50% of small enterprises fail in their first 12 months of being open.
  • 42% of small businesses fail because of a lack of demand in the market for their product.
  • Over two-thirds (66%) of small businesses significantly struggle financially.
  • 25% to 45% of businesses are no longer able to stay open after a significant financial crisis.
  • Only 25% of small businesses survive 15 years or more.

It is very important to recognize and support local businesses within your community! Not only will businesses gain the support that they need, people in your community will be able to find wonderful services to utilize and enjoy!
